Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know

Answers to the most frequent questions.

Is Wanasa free?▼

Yes! Wanasa is completely free. We have internal gems for optional additional features like gender filter (5 gems), but the core experience is full and free with 50 gems gifted at signup.

Is my identity protected?▼

100%. Wanasa uses private authentication (Firebase tech) — we don't ask you for phone number, email, or any personal information. Your nickname is the only thing that shows.

How does the matching system work?▼

Smart algorithm calculating compatibility: 10 points per shared interest + 5 points for same country + 5 points for close age + 15 point bonus for high karma (80+). The more compatibility, the better the match.

Can I make voice calls?▼

Yes! After accepting a friendship, the voice call button appears. Calls are encrypted peer-to-peer via WebRTC. You can chat and browse the app during the call via the smart call bar. Ringtone + instant notification for incoming calls.

What is Gold Membership?▼

Monthly ($10.99) or yearly ($109.99) subscription giving you: 600 gems monthly, all filters free, 5 Boosts monthly, gold badge ⭐, privacy for your data (age, country, last seen), and instant VIP support.

What is Boost?▼

A feature giving you maximum priority in matching for 30 minutes. 3-5x faster matches! Price 15 gems, or free for Gold members (5 monthly).

What are the advanced search filters?▼

Choose gender + country + age range to find the right person. Each filter costs 5 gems, or completely free for Gold members ⭐.

What is the Karma system?▼

Rating from 0 to 100 starting at 50. Accepting friendships +5, good conversations +3, reports -10. High karma = better and faster matches. 5 reports = automatic 48-hour ban.

Can I send photos and videos?▼

Yes! Send regular photos or timed (5/10/30 seconds auto-delete). Videos up to 25MB. Voice messages too. All for friends only.

What countries are supported?▼

Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, Egypt, Jordan, and Iraq.

Is screenshot blocked?▼

Yes! Screenshot and Screen Recording are automatically blocked during chats on iOS and Android.

How do I delete my account?▼

From Settings > Delete Account. All data, chats, friendships, and media are wiped from both sides immediately. Gems tied to the device remain if you return.
