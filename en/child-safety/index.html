👶 Child Safety Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

Protecting children and minors is the highest priority at Wanasa. We follow an absolute zero-tolerance policy toward any content that exploits or harms children.

Our Preventive Measures

  • Age verification: The app is available only to users aged 18 and over. Verification happens at registration through a mandatory age gate.
  • Content filtering: Smart filter for inappropriate words and content in both Arabic and English.
  • Reporting system: 6 report categories available with automatic ban after 5 reports.
  • Screenshot prevention: Additional protection during conversations.
  • Photos & audio for friends only: Sending photos and voice messages is available only after mutual friend acceptance.

Reporting Harmful Content

If you encounter any content that exploits or endangers children:

  • Use the "Report" button inside the app immediately
  • Send a detailed report to: childsafety@wanasa.club

Our Actions Upon Detecting Violations

  • Delete violating content immediately
  • Permanently ban the account and device
  • Report to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) via CyberTipline
  • Full cooperation with competent security and judicial authorities
  • Retain necessary evidence for investigations

Child safety email: childsafety@wanasa.club

We commit to responding to all child safety reports within 24 hours maximum.
